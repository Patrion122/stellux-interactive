Rolling Wreck

A 2-player co-op rally chaos game

One drives blind — no map, no UI, just voice chat. The other has the pacenotes, but the cabin has other plans: mud on the glass, doors swinging open, notebooks in flight. Same wreck of a hatchback, same countdown. Talk fast, drive faster, or fail loud enough to clip.

  • Asymmetric Co-op
  • Voice Chat
  • Physics Comedy
